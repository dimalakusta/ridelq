OPPORTUNITY

Passengers will take their rides IN CONTROL. Enter your location and destination place your “wish” price
for the ride. We will make your “wish” price for the trip available to the network of
providers.

Drivers will take their business to the highest level nearby cars or cars would presumably quote more
aggressive prices, resulting in the user securing the best cost for his trip.

Many fleets have one way trips and their cars return empty, this approach will give them a chance to bid on
offers that make sense. Bidding will be available for a short time (10-15 minutes). After bidding is “closed”,
users will be still presented with an option of choosing from all companies based on $ and ETA for the
desired destination.

product

idea

option

The idea of this platform is to create a customer App that will give people a choice of getting a ride
from many competitors based on $ vs ETA. This platform also will give the customer an option to
open bid for their trip. For example:

I’m at AIRPORT and need to go to DOWNTOWN ; I select my FROM and TO and enter the price
I’m willing to spend for that trip.

App will show a suggested price based on lowest historical average for that route. Let’s say
suggested price is $55, but I’m entering my “wish price” of $35. This gives companies a chance to
bid on this offer if they have available cars at the same destination.
